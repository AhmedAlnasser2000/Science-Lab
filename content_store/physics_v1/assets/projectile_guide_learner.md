## Mission

- Launch a projectile and track how both horizontal and vertical motion change.

## Steps

1. Set a launch speed and angle using the sliders.
2. Press **Start** to watch the arc.
3. Pause and reset to try a different angle.

## Observe

- When does the projectile reach maximum height?
- How does lowering the angle change the landing distance?
